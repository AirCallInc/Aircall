Billing History


• Billing history will show the clients’ billing details and transactions done by client i.e. billing details of the purchased units, No show payment details, Billing details of purchased parts etc.


• Admin can search details according to the client name and the date range in between the payment has done.


• Listing will show Client name, Package name, Plan name, Amount, Transaction Id, Transaction Time.



       



• In client application, From account settings > Billing history will show the client’s done transaction and its details too.


                             











Failure of Payment from the Stripe :


• For Recurring Billing of purchased unit, all the paid instalments will be shown in its detail.


• If the payment of instalment will be failed due to any reason from the stripe, then that failed payment history also will be shown in the listing of Billing History.


• The payment can be failed due to any of the following reasons :


https://stripe.com/docs/declines#decline-codes


• These are the possible reasons of failure of the payment from the Stripe.


• The notification will be sent to the client if the for expiration of the card. By clicking on the notification, the client can update the expiration details - Expiration Month & Expiration Year from edit credit card screen.


• If the client have added multiple credit cards into the system and if one credit card has expired, then the payment of instalment will be done using the other active credit card.


• But if there is the single credit card available into the client’s account and that card has expired, then stripe will provide three attempts to make payment for the failed instalment.


• Till the last attempt, if the credit card of the client has been updated then the payment will be done from the Stripe automatically.


• But if till the third attempt, the client has not updated the credit card into the Stripe account, then Admin have to manually pay the installment for failed installment.


• All the entries for failed installments will be shown in the Billing History with “Pay” button. So by clicking on “Pay” button, admin will be redirected to the AC unit subscription.


• All failed and successful transactions will be shown in the listing of Billing History.
