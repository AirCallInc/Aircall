No Show Services


• Once the service is scheduled for the unit and employee goes to the client’s place, at that time if the client is not at home then employee can’t do the service and submit the reports regarding the detail of the units.


• At that time employee will check the Work not done check box and submit the service report. that is called the “No show service” in the system.


• After clicking the work not done and submitting the report then the status for the unit will be need repair.


                 





• This no show service will be displayed in this section .


• Admin will decide whether or not to take the penalty for the now show services from the client.


• Admin will have two options to do with no show service either “Notify for no show payment” or “notify and schedule the service”.





1) Notify for no show payment:


• If admin clicks on this option then one notification will be sent to the client regarding payment amount that client has to pay for no show services.


• Once the client does the payment then the only service for that unit will be scheduled.


• If the client does not pay for that then the service for that unit will be skipped.


             








2) Notify and schedule the service:

• If the admin clicks on this option then one notification will be sent to the client to inform that client is not charged for the now show service and service for that unit will be scheduled shortly.
