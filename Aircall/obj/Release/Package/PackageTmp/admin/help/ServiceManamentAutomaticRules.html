Automatic Rules


• When any unit or any number of units will be added in the system from Client Application / Employee Application / Admin Panel, the services will be added to the database of the system for the unit when the Automatic scheduler will run.


• How many mandatory services will be added into the system for a single unit will be handled dynamically from the Admin.


• In Plan Management section, one variable is there named as “Mandatory Services”.


• “Mandatory Services” will be handled according to the PlanType for the unit.


• Total number of services for single unit = Value of “Mandatory Services”


• i.e. if Mandatory Services = 6 then 6 services will be added into the system for a single unit.


• The services are added into the database with Service Case Number.


Format Of Service Case Number :

C1-90038-S1-01 = {Client ID} - {Client’s Zip Code} - {Service Number} - {Number of service after split}


i.e. Suppose the client C1 has added unit AC1 on the address which has the zip code “90038”. So for the first service of AC1 unit is indicated as S1 and so on for another five services. 01 is indicate the number service after split for first S1 service.


How total number of Services will be added into the database :


Below are the variables which are used to add the services into the database :


(i)Mandatory Services :


• Available in Plan Management


• Indicates the total number of services will be provided to the client when any client will purchase plan.


(ii) Schedule First Service In Days :


• Available in Plan Management


• Indicates when the client will receive their first schedule service within specified days


(iii) Duration Of Plan


• Available in Plan Management


• Indicates the duration of plan in months


(iv) Time Between Two Services


• Available in Site Settings named as “Time Between Two Services”


• Indicates the time gap between two services in minutes


                 (v) PendingServiceScheduleBeforeDays


                       • Available in Site Settings


• Indicates before how many days of expected date range, service will be scheduled.


• Using above variables, total services for purchased units will be added to the database.


• First service will be scheduled in between the specified days given in “Schedule First Service In” from the date when the scheduler will run. The status of first service will be “Waiting For Approval”.


• For only first service, the employee will be assigned to the service.


• Other services for the purchased unit will be added to the database with date range.The date range is called as Expected Date in the system.


• The employee is not assigned to the other services till the status of the service is as “Pending”.


The time gap between two services of purchased unit will be calculated as below :


Next Service = (Duration - Schedule First Service) / (Mandatory Service - 1)


Consider below example :


Duration of Plan = 36 months

Mandatory Services = 6

Schedule First Service In = 30 Days

Other Service Schedule gap = 20 Days



Next Service Time Interval = (Duration - Schedule First Service) / (Mandatory Service - 1)

= (36 month - 30 days) / (6 - 1)

= 7 months = 210 Days


So other services will be scheduled after 7 months of time interval.


Next service will take into automatic scheduler are = today + Next service time interval - Other Service Schedule Gap


Note : The specified date format is taken as MM/DD/YYYY

Service Scheduling :


• As described above, the services inserted into the system.


• Below is the process how the service will be scheduled and how the employee and service time will be assigned for the service.


Please consider below example :


Plan : Residential Plan

Duration : 36 Month

Mandatory Services : 6

First Service Time Within : 30 Days

Driving Time : 20 Minutes

Service Time for First Unit : 40 Minutes

Service Time for additional Unit : 10 Minutes


1. Client Jack purchased 10 Units on 01/01/2016 using a Residential Plan


a) All units added using the same address (714 N. La Brea Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90038)


2. At the end of the day, the system scheduler will run, and system services will schedule for the new 10 units.


3. During system scheduling the following will be checked (How the Employee will be assigned to the Service) :


a) Calculate time needed to perform the service (i.e. 2-1/2hr)

b) Find the employees that are in this work area

c) According to Employee ID (sequential) choose the first

d) Verify that Employee ID 1 work schedule is open

e) If open for 2-1/2hr, schedule employee ID 1

f) If not open, check the next employee availability (ID 2), if not, check the next (ID 3) and next (ID 4) sequentially…

g) If no one is available for the same date, next, try the next day with employee ID 1 and do the same procedure

h) Highest Rated Employee, does he get any priority or not will be decided as follows :


Rating of the Employee is considered in the Automatic Rules while assigning the Employee or not is decided dynamically from the Site Settings of Admin Panel.


In the Site Settings section, “EmployeeRatingConsiderOnService” field is there.


If value is yes, an employee who has highest rating will be considered first. And if No, the rating of the employee will not be considered while assigning the employee.


4. Total 6 Services will be added into the database. Only first service will be scheduled when the system scheduler will run. Employee, Parts and service time assigned for 1st Service only. For other 5 services, only Expected service date range will be assigned (No Employee + No Parts + No Service Scheduling Time)

Calculations for Scheduling Service :

• System calculates that the service requires 2-1/2 hr.


• Service Time Calculation :


S1= Driving Time + Time to Service first unit + (Time to service additional units X quantity of additional units).

             = 20 + 40 + (9 * 10)

                                = 20 + 40 + 90

                                = 150 Minutes = 2-1/2hr.


• On Client Unit address, the system found that there are 2 employees (Scott and William) Work Area matching with client address.


• Scott has Employee ID 1, thereby system will check his schedule first.


• Scott is free on 01/06/2016 between 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM and thereby the system will insert for him the new service job schedule.


• Now the service is scheduled, the system will send the client an Approval of Service notification.


• If client approves that service, then Employee Scott will go to Jack unit address on 01/06/2016 and service the 10 units.


• The status of the service will change to “Scheduled” from “Waiting For Approval” once the client has accepted the service from the Approval notification of service.


• If the employee is free yet required parts are unavailable, the service will move to pending state. System will insert attempt log with the reasons. (i.e Parts Filter-20X10X10 is out of stock, service cannot be commenced.)


• If none of the employees are available between 01/01/2016 to 02/01/2016, the service will move to “Pending" state with detailed attempt log.


For other services (S2 to S6) schedule calculation are as below:


S2 Service Expected Start Date : 09/01/2016

S2 Service Expected End Date : 10/01/2016


• Means 2nd service will be scheduled between above two dates with sequential priority.


• On 08/01/2016 Scheduler will run and schedule the service S2 between 09/01/2016 and 10/01/2016.


• Before how many days of Expected Start Date the scheduler will run will be handled dynamically from Site Settings.


• If the employee Jack schedule is open for 2-1/2hr. on 09/01/2016 and also parts are in stock, then S2 will be added to his schedule on 09/01/2016.


• Process for assigning the employee to other services (S2 to S6) are the same as we have assigned employee on S1.


• Thus the scheduling of the services will be done.

Reschedule & Request For Service :


• The automatic rules of service scheduling while rescheduling of the service or request a new service are different than the other Plan services.


• We have two-time slots for rescheduling and requesting of the service.


• These two-time slots are coming from the Plan Management from the Admin Panel. These slots are different according to the plan types.


• If the client has selected the first time slot and employee is not free that time slot but free for the second time slot then requested/ rescheduled/ canceled service will be schedule at the second time slot for the date selected by client.



Suppose,

First Time Slot : 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM = 4 Hours

Second Time Slot : 12:00 PM to 4:00 PM = 4 Hours


• The scheduled service / Requested service have 2 units with Industrial Plan.


Time to service single unit = 240 minutes = 4 hours

Time to service other units = 120 minutes = 2 hours

Driving Time = 20 minutes


So the total time to service 2 units = 240 + 120 + 20 = 380 minutes = 6.33 = 7 hours


Employee Working Time = 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM


• Employee working time will be taken from the Employee MGMT in Admin Panel.


For Time Slot 1 :

• If the request has no. of units for which the employee needs time more than 4 hours (more time than the total time of slot 1) OR if the scheduled request has the no. of units for which the employee needs time more than 4 Hours to do service and if the client has selected first time slot, then as per below process the service will be scheduled.


• As per the above timings, the employee needs 7 hours to complete the service and the client has selected 8 AM to        12 PM time for rescheduling.


• So we will assign two employees for this same service in between 8:00 AM to 12PM time slot.


For Time Slot 2 :


• As per the above timings, the employee needs 7 hours to complete the service, then we will not allow the client to select the second time slot from the Reschedule and Request for service.
